Killer Flatbread

Description

A perfect flat bread to pair with stew and mead. Eat it plain, or dip it in your mead or stew for a little punch of flavor

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  • In a large skull, mix the flour and salt.
  • Slowly add water and stir until a soft dough forms. Kneed until smooth.
  • Divide the dough into 6 equal pieces the roll them out into round flatbreads.
  • Cook on a flat pan, 2-3 minutes per side.
  • Serve with mead and honey.
